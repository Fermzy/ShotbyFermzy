ABOUT
SHOTBYFERMZY

ShotbyFermzy is a Nigeria-based photographer and visual storyteller with four years of experience crafting images that go beyond the frame. Rooted in African culture and driven by a bold editorial eye, ShotbyFermzy's work blends high-fashion sensibility with raw human truth — turning every shoot into a narrative that resonates far beyond the shutter click.

From vibrant wax-print backdrops to the quiet power of a gaze, every image is intentional, cultural, and unmistakably ShotbyFermzy. Available for international brand campaigns, editorial features, and model portfolio shoots.
